National SCCA Solo Website

Base vehicle must be production based chasis from the manufacturer

Category Specific Allowable Modifications

  • Wheels diameter Up to "Plus 1 " of OE wheel spec
  • Wheel offset +/- 7.oo mm or +/- 0.275 inches of OE spec
  • Tires: Minimum Tread wear rating of 200 UTOG
  • Exhaust: Cat back aftermarket allowed
  • Suspension Shock Absorber Only aftermarket part allowed is a single adjustable shock absorber
  • Suspension Sway bar Only one sway bar can be different from the OE spec, either front or rear is allowed
  • Complete rules for Street Category can be found on section 13 pages 73 - 83 of the SCCA Solo Rule Book.

    Base vehicle must be production spec from the manufacturer

    Allowable class core modifications beyond street category rules

    • No permanent alteration to OE body panels allowed.
    • Bolt on performance parts allowed.
    • Suspension modifications allowed as long as OE suspension mounting points are used.
    • Multi adjustable shock absorbers allowed
    • Lowering springs and multi height adjustable "coilover systems " allowed
    • Wheel and tire upgrade allowed, but minimum tire treadwear rating cannot be below 200 UTOQ.
    • Suspension bushings can be replaced with polyurethane bushings
    • "Big Brake " kits allowed
    • Complete rules for Street Touring Category can be found on section 14 pages 85 - 96 of the SCCA Solo Rule Book.
